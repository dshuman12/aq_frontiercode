# Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free
experience for everyone.

Acceptable behavior includes:
- Empathy and kindness toward other people.
- Respect for differing opinions and experiences.
- Constructive feedback.

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Sexualized language or attention.
- Trolling, insulting, derogatory comments, personal attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.

Reports may be sent to **conduct@kindling-tools.example**. All
complaints are reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
